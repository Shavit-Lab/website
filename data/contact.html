Collaborations

We are always happy to collaborate with other researchers in machine learning, connectomics, and neuroscience! Please contact either Nir Shavit using the information below or one of the students. If you are applying to the MIT EECS PhD program and are interested in joining our lab, please apply through the department directly and list Nir as one of your potential mentors. If you are a post-doc or and undergraduate student and would like to join our lab, please reach out to Nir directly.
